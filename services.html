Services

Organic visibility across search and AI.

SEO strategy built for how search actually works today. Google, Bing, AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity. One integrated approach that covers all of it.

Search has changed. The strategy has to as well.

Traditional SEO is still the foundation. But ranking in blue links is no longer the whole game. AI platforms are now answering questions, recommending brands, and citing sources without users ever clicking a result. If your content is not visible in those systems, you are missing half the picture.

Traditional SEO
Ranking on Google and Bing through technical foundations, content quality, and authority signals. Still essential. Still the base.
AEO / GEO
Answer Engine Optimisation and Generative Engine Optimisation. Getting your brand cited in AI-generated responses across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and AI Overviews.
The integrated approach
Both channels feed each other. Strong organic SEO signals improve AI citation likelihood. Strong AI visibility builds brand authority that flows back into search rankings.
